Getting it to you

How delivery works.

Delivery

Curbside delivery

Delivery is priced by straight-line distance from Bakersfield: free within 10 mi, $150 (10–30), $200 (30–50), $250 (50–80), $310 (80–100). Beyond 100 mi is pickup or a custom freight quote. Carriers deliver to the curb or driveway — inspect and note visible carton or pallet damage before signing.

Access

Confirm truck access

Tell us first if the street is narrow, gated, steep, rural, under construction, or hard for a freight truck to reach.

Storage

Keep cartons protected

Store flooring flat, indoors, and away from standing water, direct sun, and job-site debris until installation.

Timing

Wait to schedule labor

Confirm the full order arrived in good condition before scheduling installers or removing existing flooring.

Returns

Uninstalled, unopened, and approved first.

We want you to love your new flooring. Inspect your order as soon as it arrives, and contact us before sending anything back.

Return eligibility

Returns may be accepted only when all of the following apply:

  • Return is requested within 30 days of delivery.
  • Flooring has not been installed.
  • Product is in original, unopened packaging.
  • Product is in new, resalable condition.
  • Return has an approved Return Authorization (RMA).

A 25% restocking fee applies. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs.

Non-returnable items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Installed flooring
  • Opened or partial cartons
  • Custom or special-order products
  • Clearance or final sale items
  • Trim, moldings, adhesives, underlayment, and samples

Damaged or incorrect orders

Please inspect your shipment upon delivery. If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us within 48 hours so we can help make it right.

Manufacturing defects

Do not install flooring you believe has a manufacturing defect. Contact us immediately. Installed flooring is considered accepted for visible condition and may not qualify for replacement under the Limited Warranty.
